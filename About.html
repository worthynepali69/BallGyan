About Ball Gyan

Ball Gyan is a modern sports news portal dedicated to revolutionizing sports journalism for the youth of Nepal as of March 30, 2025. We provide real-time updates, engaging content, and a seamless experience, celebrating Nepal’s rich sports culture.

Our uniqueness lies in integrating advanced sports data analysis, offering deep insights into performance metrics, and delivering exclusive insider news on Nepali sports like cricket, football, and volleyball, including updates on governing bodies, players, and leagues such as the Nepal Super League.

Meet Our Founder

Founded by Aayush Bashyal, an IT student based in Auckland, New Zealand, Ball Gyan reflects his passion for technology and sports. Aayush’s vision is to empower Nepali youths with a modern, data-driven platform that puts Nepali sports on the global stage, fostering a community of informed and inspired fans.
